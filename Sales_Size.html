Size vs Sales

The scatter plot below shows the relationship between ths size of the store and the total sales. Each points represents each stores. The linear line that goes through the plot shows that the size and the sales have a positive relationship which means that bigger the size of the store higher the sales are.

The training scores and testing scores shows that our modeling is reliable. The residual plots are centered towards the line and the training data points and test data points are close to each other which shows a good testing results. It seems reasonable to believe the result because if the size of the store is big, they have more items to sell. As seen in the scatter plot, it is not always the case, but they have a hight probability that it will happen.
